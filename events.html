Battle of The Canvas - Oakland

Appearing for the first time in Oakland, the Battle Of The Canvas® originally produced in Philadelphia, continues to be a means to expose, support and celebrate local artistry in cities throughout the country. Simone Banks, co-founder of WAXXED and Andre Jones have partnered to bring the live painting festival to Oakland, working hand in hand to provide access and visibility to some of the most talented artists the city has to offer. All artists will compete for a $500 Grand Prize, $350 in Gift Cards donated by Flax Art Store and city wide exposure.

Date:

August 15th, 4 PM
